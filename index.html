# about

I spend my days getting transformer models to predict when trucks will show up (harder than it sounds) and my evenings wondering whether consciousness is computable.

Before logistics I was at Razorpay routing payments with reinforcement learning, and at ShareChat figuring out face detection on phones that cost less than a textbook. The through-line is: I like problems where the real world refuses to cooperate with your model.

Outside of work I read too much (economics, philosophy, history, whatever's lying around), run two Substacks about complex systems and the strange loops in everything, and occasionally get lost in papers about foundational models and multimodal architectures.

# research

arXiv:2112.13706

Multi-Image Visual Question Answering

BTech project at IIT Dharwad. Proposed a new dataset for VQA with multiple image inputs and a single ground truth. The final model used ResNet + RCNN image features with BERT embeddings, inspired by stacked attention networks. Achieved 39% word accuracy and 99% image accuracy on CLEVER+TinyImagenet.

arXiv:2101.05806

Video Captioning on ActivityNet

Introduced WAFTM (Weighted Additive Fusion Transformer with Memory Augmented Encoders) for video captioning. Used a novel feature fusion method and memory-augmented transformer encoders. CIDEr of 92.4 on MSVD, METEOR of 0.091 on ActivityNet.
