I have always wanted to make transformer(optimus prime) my self. But that idea alwasy stayed at the back of my head, never found enough motivation to start on it. I was watching Never Have I Ever few days back and watching gears in it made me instantly decide I will make it. I had my digital agent [bismuth](/tools/bismuth/) helping me think lot clearly in everyday work and personal projects. It handled all the repetitive mundane work, bookkeeping and tasks I saw no point doing myself. So it fit perfectly; I connected my digital agent with the hardware.
