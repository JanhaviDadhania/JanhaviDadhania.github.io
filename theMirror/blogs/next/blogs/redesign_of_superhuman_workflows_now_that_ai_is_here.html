redesign of superhuman workflows now that AI is here
current workflows were designed with the assumption that cognitive agents proficient in natural langugage cognition does not exist.
But now that they are here, the workflows WILL change. how do we want our newer systems to be?
state of current systems
this is how any engineering division in company looks like. CXOs decide where does company want to move. low level executive decides what do we need to do to acheive that goal. That passes down to engineering directors(maangers of managers). engineering directors go on meetings with all managers(experts of their own areas for example data-science, frontend, backend, devOps etc). Till here most work happens in documents, sheets or niche personalised softwares.
Individual team managers work involve lots of meetings , documents , excel sheets to track .
Staff and principle engineers works at the same level but more on technical side. Even their daily life goes around same tools.
Under them are L1 L2 and L3 engineers who works with long list of software tools. slack, github, IDEs, jenkins, argoCD, grafana, looker, jira, confluence, terraform, snowflake, databricks.....
role tasks & tools
L1 L2 L3 engineers it works on my machine. IDEs, personal scripts, personal notes, personal todos, docker desktop, local kube clusters, GitHub, Slack, Google docs, Chrome.

product managers PRDs, product analytics softwares, calls with engineers, managers and staff. sits in the tension between shipping pressure and engineering constraints.
deploy / infra jenkinds, github actions, argoCD, terraform, graphana, cloud management.
alerts / ops pagers, datadog, on call duties.

most tools junior devs use can now be connected to LLMs. So most things junior engineers do can be done by agents.(not just a guess anymore, its real).
Every junior engineers productivity will increase many many x goign forward. We will either have very few junior engineers working in companies or oen junior engineer might be producing 5 engineers output. THIS WILL DIRECTLY AFFECT EVERYONE WORKING ABOVE THEM.
For everyone above junior engineers two things will happend. first, they will also have agent as an assistant and second they will have lot less people to manager and lot more work to review.
new design
junior engineers will now instead of working with tools themselves, monitor agents working on them. they might monitor 5-6 agents at a time and fill in wherever agent requires human judgement. They will be instructing and observing more instead of doing.
So tools that lets us instruct(message apps, audio message apps) and observe(dashboard, analytics, alerts app) will see themselves getting integrated more.
tables devices and microphone devices which are perfect for instruction and observations will be in demand. (not just hardware, even instruct and observe softwares will be produced more).
